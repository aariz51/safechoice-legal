Account & data deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your SafeChoice account and the data associated with it. SafeChoice does not require a name, email, or password — each install uses an automatically created anonymous account, so there is no login to sign into to delete data yourself. To have your data erased, follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Email aarizrasheed2345@gmail.com from any email address.
  2. Use the subject line “Delete my SafeChoice data”.
  3. To help us locate your account, include the approximate date you started using the app and the device platform (iPhone or Android). If you are unsure, email us anyway and we will help you.
We confirm and complete deletion requests within 30 days of receiving them, and email you when it is done.

What gets deleted

When you request deletion, we permanently remove the following from our systems:

  • Your anonymous account identifier
  • Onboarding preferences (goals, dietary focus)
  • Your full scan history and saved results
  • Product photos you submitted for analysis
  • Your AI assistant chat messages

What may be retained

We may retain a minimal record of a subscription transaction where we are legally required to (for example, tax and accounting obligations, or fraud prevention). These records do not include your scan history, photos, or chat messages, and are kept only for as long as the law requires. Subscription billing records held by Apple or Google are governed by their own policies.

Subscriptions

Deleting your data does not automatically cancel an active subscription. To cancel billing, manage your subscription in your App Store (Apple) or Google Play (Android) account settings before, or in addition to, requesting deletion.
